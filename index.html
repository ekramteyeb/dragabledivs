The European Union's Digital Services Act

The European Union's Digital Services Act (DSA) will come into effect this week, requiring major tech companies like Google, Facebook, and TikTok to follow new regulations aimed at keeping users safe online and preventing the spread of harmful content. The DSA will apply to 19 platforms, including social media platforms like Instagram and Snapchat, online marketplaces like Amazon and Alibaba AliExpress, mobile app stores like Google Play and Apple's App Store, and others such as Google Maps and Wikipedia.
